Why Futura is Great!

Futura is one of the worlds most recognized and appreciated typefaces.

Created in 1927 by Paul Renner. Futura was developed as a geometric sans serif typeface. It has become one of the most widley used and known typefaces in the world. Taking on the apperance of simple shapes and strokes. It comprises an entire family ranging from bold to oblique.
